CubeCell™ is a Heltec product line for LoRa/LoRaWAN nodes, based on ASR605x (ASR6501/6502) with integrated PSoC® 4000 MCU and SX1262. It fully supports Arduino®, runs LoRaWAN stably, and easily connects to lithium batteries and solar panels. HTCC-AB02 is a developer-friendly board for quick communication solution verification.

## Product characteristics
- Arduino-compatible, CE & FCC certified
- ASR605x (MCU + SX1262), supporting LoRaWAN 1.0.2
- Ultra-low power: only 3.5µA in deep sleep
- Power & solar management: battery interface, protection, auto switching
- Rich interfaces: long-range RF, CP2102 USB, 0.96" OLED display
