## Product characteristics
- Arduino-compatible, CE & FCC certified
- ASR605x (MCU + SX1262), with LoRaWAN 1.0.2 support
- Ultra-low power design, only 21 µA in deep sleep
- Comprehensive power management: battery interface, protection, solar charging (5.5–7V)
- Enhanced features: long-range RF, CP2102 USB, 0.96" OLED, and Air530 GPS (GPS/Beidou dual-mode)
