The **Meshtower V2 series** is available in two power variants to support different wireless communication requirements. The **Meshtower V2** is the standard-power version with a maximum RF output power of **21 ±1 dBm**, while the **Meshtower V2H** is the high-power version with a maximum RF output power of **28 ±1 dBm**, providing enhanced wireless coverage and improved long-range communication performance in more demanding deployment environments.

## Product characteristicss


- 3X2800mAh high-capacity lithium batteries.
- Supports 18~24V solar input, DC 18-24V input and USB-C PD3.0(20V) input.
- Dual Support for LoRa & Bluetooth.
- IP66-rated metal enclosure, waterproof & flame-retardant.
