Capsule Sensor V3 is a compact, portable LoRa/LoRaWAN device powered by the ESP32-S3 and SX1262. Featuring a modular design for compatibility with various sensors and supporting WirelessBoot technology, it is waterproof, lightweight, and equipped with a 250 mAh rechargeable battery. It is ideal for precise monitoring across diverse scenarios, including musical instruments, wine cellars, greenhouses, and more.

## Product characteristics
- Integrates LoRa, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth with built-in antennas; Meshtastic compatible
- ESP32-S3 + SX1262, built-in 250 mAh battery, magnetic charging, and LED indicator
- High-strength housing, IP65 rated, lightweight for outdoor use
- Supports Arduino, PlatformIO, and other mainstream development platforms
- Reserved BTB connector; supports Heltec Wireless Boot for remote firmware updates and debugging
