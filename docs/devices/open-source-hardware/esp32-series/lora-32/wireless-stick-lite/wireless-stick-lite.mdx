The HTIT-WSL is a development board that integrates Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and LoRa connectivity. Unlike the WiFi LoRa32, this version is designed without an OLED display to better suit customers who do not require it. Powered by the ESP32-S3FN8 MCU and Semtech SX1262 LoRa transceiver, the board offers full compatibility with Arduino®, making it easy for users to perform secondary development and create a wide range of applications.

## Product characteristics

- ESP32-S3FN8 MCU, support Wi-Fi, LoRa and BLE
- SX1262 LoRa node chip, compatible with Meshtastic
- Integrated CP2102 USB to serial port chip
- Features Type-C USB, multiple protections, and built-in battery management
- With good RF circuit design and basic low-power design (sleep current ≤800uA)
