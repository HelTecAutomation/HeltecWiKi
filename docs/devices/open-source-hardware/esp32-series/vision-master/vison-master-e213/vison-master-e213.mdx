The Vision Master E213 (HT-VME213) is a versatile 2.13-inch black-and-white E-Ink development kit with Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and LoRa connectivity, supporting multiple frameworks for creating applications such as electronic labels, identity tags, and open-source projects like Meshtastic.

  </a>
</div>}

## Product characteristics

- ESP32-S3R8 MCU, support Wi-Fi and BLE
- LoRa module is optional, compatible with Meshtastic
- Low power consumption, 20uA in deep sleep, continuous display for 180 days after power outage
- Onboard 2.13 inch 250 x 122 pixels display, support for partial refresh
- 2*SH2.0-4P sensor interface is perfectly compatible with QuickLink series sensors
